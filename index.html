About

Accredited by UGC and approved by AICTE, Techno India University, West Bengal is a fully fledged university with numerous study programmes in science, humanities, commerce, engineering, architecture, management, law and health sciences. The University provides world class infrastructure equipped with modern technology enabled teaching- learning tools and vigorous industry-driven curricula.
